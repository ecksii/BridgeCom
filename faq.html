  • Q

    Where will BridgeCom be held

    A

    On a farm ~10/15 minutes out of the north east of Cambridge . The exact location will be sent to ticket holders closer to the time.

    Q

    How do I get there?

    A

    The venue is a 15/20 minutes cycle from Cambridge center. For those who live further afield, it is a 20 minute walk from Cambridge north station.
    There is parking for those who drive.

    Q

    Do I have to buy a ticket?

    A

    Entry is on a donation basis with a sliding scale so you can pay what is affordable to you.

    Q

    What will the money be used for?

    A

    We will use the donations to pay for services such as toilet and generator rentals, as well as resources such as food for the BBQ and other necessities.
    A report of the finances will be avaiable here after the event.

    Q

    Can I stay overnight?

    A

    Yes! There will be space to camp at the site. Camper vans are also allowed.

    Q

    I've never been to a burn, can I come?

    A

    Yes! Everyone is welcome :) Check out our new burner page it this is your first time ;)

    Q

    Can I bring kids, dogs and other animals?

    A

    There are some potential health and saftey risks such as open fire. So please conact us if you are considering bringing your children or pets.

    Q

    Will there be food?

    A

    Yes there will be! We are running a pot-luck teddy bears' picnic lunch and invite attendees to bring a vegan dish to share! We will be facilitating a vegan BBQ in the evening

    Q

    Can I bring non-vegan food on site?

    A

    No, sorry. This is a vegan farm and the land owners have specified that no non-vegan things be brought onto their property as part our terms of use.
