One of the most important of the 11 principles of a burn is "communal effort"



These events are run entirely by volunteers and we need YOU to help make BridgeCom happen!



There are lots of ways you can get involved:

  • Create art to add to our psychedelic gallery
  • Share your talents in a workshop, performance or talk
  • DJ a set in our dance tent
  • Bring a dish for the teddy bears' picnic
  • Help us with decor and infrastructure by bringing something from our wishlist
  • Fill one of the outstanding lead roles
  • Decorate our beautiful site with the decor team
  • Come by on the 27th to help with build activities
  • Stay behind on the 29th to help us strike down after the event
  • Volunteer for shifts on the day as part of the kitchen, welfare, gate or fire saftey teams
